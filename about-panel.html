About confbox

ConfBox - Mobile platform for delivering the comprehensive information about the conferences and events straight to the users' pockets. It includes information about event and organizaer, agenda, detailed schedule, talks, participants and their organizations, locations and much more.

App version: 2014.08.16.

Developed by

Laboratory for Development and Transfer of Mobile Technologies

University of Zagreb, Faculty of Organization and Informatics, Varaždin
